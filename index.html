Under the Hood

Science-driven,
not gimmick-driven

MealOptimizer is built on real nutritional science and advanced AI — not calorie guesses and generic meal templates. FP-Growth pattern mining, Fuzzy Logic, and Genetic Algorithms work together under the hood.

🧬

Genetic Algorithm Optimization

Evolves meal combinations across generations using selection, crossover, and mutation to find globally optimal daily plans for your exact macros and calories.

🔬

Mifflin-St Jeor BMR Formula

Industry-standard basal metabolic rate calculation, combined with activity multipliers and personalized TDEE adjustments — not rough estimates.

🌫️

Fuzzy Logic Inference Engine

BMI membership functions (underweight, normal, overweight, obese) drive nuanced goal recommendations and calorie deficit/surplus magnitudes — no binary thresholds.

💪

BW-Based Macro Splits

Protein and fat targets calculated per kg of body weight — adapting automatically for standard users and athletes in Muscle Mode with separate lose/gain/maintain splits.

🔗

FP-Growth Frequent Pattern Mining

Our entire cuisine and dish dataset was built using FP-Growth association rule mining — discovering which foods, ingredients, and meal combinations naturally co-occur across 15 cuisines and 200+ dishes, powering smarter, culturally-accurate meal suggestions.
